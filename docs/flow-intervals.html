1. Introduction

Welcome to FlowIntervals! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. By using FlowIntervals, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

FlowIntervals stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Timer Settings: Your preferred focus durations, break lengths, and timer configurations
  • Session History: Your completed focus sessions, timestamps, and session statistics
  • Daily Progress: Your streaks, total focus time, and achievement data
  • App Preferences: Sound settings, notifications preferences, and theme choices

Important: This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable analytics (see below).

2.2 Anonymous Analytics (Optional)

With your explicit consent, we collect anonymous usage analytics through Mixpanel to improve FlowIntervals. This includes:

  • App Usage Patterns: Which features you use and how often
  • Session Metrics: Anonymous statistics about focus session completion rates and durations
  • Settings Preferences: Which timer configurations are most popular (anonymous)
  • Technical Information: App version, device platform (iOS/Android), and language preference
  • Anonymous User ID: A randomly generated identifier that cannot be linked to you personally

Analytics help us understand:

  • Which features are most valuable to users
  • Where users encounter difficulties
  • How to improve the app experience
  • Whether new features are being adopted

You can disable analytics at any time in Settings.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Consent Management

  • First Launch: You'll be asked for consent before any analytics are collected
  • Change Anytime: Enable or disable analytics in the Settings panel
